How Our Website Works

  1. Start by creating an account or logging in if you already have one.
  2. Take a look at our huge collection of PCs and their specifications.
  3. Utilize our filters to specify exactly what you're looking for in a PC and pick your desired budget.

Why Do We Use 3DMARK?

3DMark has established itself as a highly reputable benchmarking tool renowned for its wide variety of tests, which effectively assess the performance of CPUs and GPUs and more. It offers a wide range of realistic gaming scenarios and stress tests ensuring that the results obtained are trustworthy and accurate. Once the benchmarks are complete, it validates the score ensuring autheticity. Additionally, 3DMark allows users to compare their system's performance against a vast database of results, enabling them to make well-informed decisions when it comes to selecting the most suitable CPUs and GPUs, articularly for gaming purposes. User's can also download the 3DMark app and see how their own PC performs, relative to others.
